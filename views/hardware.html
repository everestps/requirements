Hardware

Goal: To describe the parts of a computer and what they specifically do and how a computer works.

Required Topics:

  • What is a motherboard and what does it do?
  • What is a CPU and what does it do?
  • What is RAM and what does it?
  • What is a hard drive and what does it do?
  • How do the different components communicate with one another?
  • What are the keyboard, mouse, and monitor and what do they do?

Required Content:

  • Navigation Bar (Must link to all of your other pages)
  • A picture for each piece of hardware
  • At least 2 of the following: An Ordered List, an Unordered List, a Link, a Table
  • CSS styling including a background

Optional Ideas:

  • What is a videocard and what does it do?
  • What is a soundcard and what does it do?
  • What are the pieces of the hardware?
  • What are the Specifications of the hardware?
  • Anything else you want!
